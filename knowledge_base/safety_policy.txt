Ride Safety Incident Policy

Overview

Safety is a core priority for the ride-hailing platform. The company enforces strict safety policies to ensure that both riders and drivers have a secure and respectful experience during every trip.

Safety incidents are taken seriously and are investigated promptly by the platform’s Safety and Trust team.

Types of Safety Incidents

Safety incidents may include, but are not limited to:

• reckless or dangerous driving
• harassment or inappropriate behavior
• verbal threats or physical violence
• unsafe or poorly maintained vehicles
• discrimination or abusive conduct
• driving under the influence
• unauthorized passengers in the vehicle
• violation of platform community guidelines

Reporting a Safety Issue During a Trip

If a rider feels unsafe during a trip, the following steps should be taken immediately:

1. Use the in-app Safety Assistance button.
2. Contact local emergency services if the situation requires immediate help.
3. Share the trip status with trusted contacts through the safety toolkit.
4. End the trip if it is safe to do so.

The platform provides real-time safety tools that allow riders to access help quickly.

Reporting a Safety Issue After a Trip

Riders can also report safety concerns after the trip has ended through the app’s Help or Support section.

When submitting a safety report, riders should provide:

• trip details and trip ID
• the driver's name or vehicle information
• a detailed description of the incident
• any supporting evidence such as photos or messages

Providing accurate details helps the safety team conduct a faster investigation.

Safety Investigation Process

When a safety report is submitted, the platform’s Safety Investigation Team reviews several sources of information, including:

• trip history and trip timeline
• rider complaint details
• driver behavior and rating history
• GPS route and location data
• previous safety reports related to the driver
• communication between rider and driver during the trip

The investigation team evaluates all available information to determine whether platform safety policies were violated.

Possible Actions and Enforcement

Depending on the severity of the incident, the platform may take one or more of the following actions:

• issue warnings to the driver
• require driver retraining
• temporarily suspend the driver account
• permanently deactivate the driver account
• cooperate with law enforcement authorities if necessary

Severe safety violations may result in immediate account suspension while the investigation is ongoing.

Continuous Safety Improvements

The platform continuously reviews and updates safety policies, driver screening processes, and safety technology to improve the safety of the ride-hailing experience.

Riders and drivers are encouraged to report safety concerns to help maintain a secure community for all users.
