Rider Support and Issue Resolution Policy

Overview

The ride-hailing platform provides support services to help riders resolve issues that may occur before, during, or after a trip. Rider complaints are reviewed using trip data, payment records, and platform policies to ensure fair and accurate resolutions.

Common Rider Issues

1. Incorrect Fare Charges

Riders may report that they were charged more than expected for a trip. Fare differences may occur due to factors such as route changes, waiting time charges, toll fees, or dynamic surge pricing.

When reviewing fare complaints, the support team analyzes:
• trip receipt and pricing breakdown
• GPS route data
• trip duration and distance
• applicable surge pricing conditions
• toll or additional charges

If an incorrect charge is identified, the rider may receive a refund or fare adjustment.

2. Driver Behavior Complaints

Riders can report concerns related to driver professionalism or behavior during a trip. Examples include:

• unsafe or reckless driving
• inappropriate or disrespectful communication
• harassment or discrimination
• violation of platform community guidelines

Each report is reviewed and documented in the driver's safety record. Repeated violations may lead to warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent deactivation of the driver account.

3. Trip Cancellation Issues

Cancellation fees may apply when a rider cancels a trip after a driver has already accepted the ride and started traveling toward the pickup location.

Support teams review:
• driver acceptance time
• rider cancellation time
• driver arrival progress
• platform cancellation policy

If the cancellation fee was applied incorrectly, a refund may be issued.

4. App or Payment Issues

Riders may occasionally experience technical or payment-related issues, including:

• payment failures
• duplicate charges
• incomplete trip payments
• app booking errors

These cases are reviewed using:
• payment transaction logs
• payment gateway records
• trip confirmation data
• system error logs

If a technical error is confirmed, the rider may receive a refund or credit.

5. Lost Items During Trips

If a rider forgets an item in the vehicle, they can report the lost item through the app's Help or Support section.

The support team will:
• notify the driver about the reported item
• attempt to reconnect the rider with the driver
• arrange communication for item recovery when possible

Recovery of lost items depends on driver availability and the nature of the item.

Support Investigation Process

When a rider submits a complaint or issue report, the support system analyzes multiple data sources, including:

• trip ID and trip history
• GPS route and trip timeline
• driver activity logs
• fare calculation records
• payment transaction data
• rider complaint details

Support agents review the available data to determine whether platform policies were followed and what resolution should be applied.

Refund and Compensation Policy

Refunds or compensation may be issued in cases such as:

• incorrect fare calculation
• technical or system errors
• driver misconduct or policy violations
• incorrect cancellation fees
• duplicate payment charges

Refunds may be processed back to the rider's original payment method or issued as platform credits depending on the situation.

Safety Reporting

Riders can report safety concerns during or after a trip through the in-app safety tools or the support section.

Serious safety incidents are escalated to the platform’s Trust and Safety team for further investigation and possible enforcement actions.

Continuous Support Improvement

The platform continuously monitors rider feedback, complaint trends, and support performance to improve the overall rider experience and ensure fair resolution of issues.
