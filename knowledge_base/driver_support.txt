Driver Payment and Earnings Policy

Overview

Drivers earn income by completing trips through the ride-hailing platform. Driver earnings are calculated automatically by the platform based on multiple trip-related factors and pricing policies.

The platform ensures transparent payment calculations and provides drivers with access to trip receipts and earnings summaries through the driver app.

Driver Earnings Calculation

Driver earnings for each completed trip are calculated using the following components:

• Base fare – a fixed starting amount for every trip
• Distance fare – payment based on the distance traveled
• Time fare – payment based on the total trip duration
• Surge pricing multiplier – additional earnings during periods of high demand
• Tolls or additional charges when applicable

The final trip fare is calculated automatically by the platform’s pricing system.

Driver Payout Schedule

Driver payments are typically processed on a weekly basis through the driver's registered payout method. Depending on the region, drivers may also have access to instant payout options that allow them to withdraw earnings earlier.

The driver earnings summary in the driver app provides:

• completed trip earnings
• surge bonuses
• rider tips
• adjustments or corrections
• total weekly earnings

Common Driver Payment Issues

Drivers may occasionally report issues related to trip payments or earnings calculations. Common issues include:

1. Missing Trip Payments  
A driver may believe that payment for a completed trip was not recorded.

2. Incorrect Surge Calculation  
Drivers may report that surge pricing was not applied correctly during high-demand periods.

3. Delayed Payouts  
Drivers may experience delays in receiving weekly payouts due to payment processing or banking issues.

4. Rider Cancellation Compensation  
Drivers may be eligible for compensation if a rider cancels a trip after the driver has already traveled toward the pickup location.

Payment Investigation Process

When a driver reports a payment issue, the support system reviews several sources of information, including:

• trip ID and trip details
• GPS trip route and timeline
• driver activity logs
• fare calculation records
• surge pricing data during the trip
• rider cancellation timestamps

Support agents analyze this data to verify whether the earnings were calculated correctly.

Possible Resolutions

Depending on the investigation results, the platform may take actions such as:

• correcting fare calculations
• issuing payment adjustments
• providing cancellation compensation
• resolving payout delays
• updating driver earnings records

Drivers are notified through the driver app once the payment review is completed.

Driver Support Access

Drivers can report payment or earnings issues through the driver support portal available in the driver app. Support agents review submitted complaints and respond with the outcome of the investigation.

Continuous Improvement

The platform continuously reviews payment systems and driver feedback to improve payment transparency and ensure fair earnings for all drivers.
